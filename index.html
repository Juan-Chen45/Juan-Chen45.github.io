I am a first-year Ph.D. student researching Computer Vision (CV) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), advised by Prof. Lei Zhang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in computer science at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), where I was honored to work with Prof. Xuan Song. I was a research assistant at SUSTech-UTokyo Joint Research Center on Super Smart Cities, where I worked with Prof.Zipei Fan.

Research Interest

My research interest broadly lies in the Deep Learning techniques for real-world applications. In recent years, my research has specifically focused on the following directions:

  • Low-level vision (e.g., image super-resolution, image restoration).
  • Spatial-temporal data mining (e.g., traffic forecasting, travel time estimation).
